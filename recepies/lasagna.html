Classic Lasagna with Red + White sauce and Parmesean Cheese

This is a short recepie on how to make a Lasagna with the ingredients listed above.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna type Pasta
  • Red Sauce/Tomato Sauce
  • White Sauce/Alfredo Sauce
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Grinded Meat

Steps

  1. Make the meat Sauce
  2. Make the Ricotta Cheese mixture
  3. Boil the pasta
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Cool
  7. Enjoy!
