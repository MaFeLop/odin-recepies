Classic Lasagna with Red + White sauce and Parmesean Cheese

This is a short recepie on how to make a Lasagna with the ingredients listed above.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Tomato Paste
  • Seasoning
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the Beans sauce
  2. Cook the Rice
  3. Prepare 2 pots, one for the Rice and the other for the beans
  4. Add together
  5. Let it Cool a bit
  6. Enjoy!
